Why TakeTask?

Information Verified

Our collected Information will be verified by time-stamp, GPS marker and photo/ video verifying system.

Optimized algorithm

With OPRD algorithm and reward optimization, maximize your reward by recommending tasks and suggesting upload timing.

Dashboard for Clients

Collected Information can be viewed in Client Dashboard with statistics and charts. Clients can raised their tasks here.

Features

Different form others money-earning application, TakeTask provides "Friends Zone" for agents to take tasks with friends, get the rewards together.

You can exchange the rewards among your friends, such as e-coupons and TakeTask points!

Check your TakeTask ranking among your friends, see if you are in leading position!

Invite your friends to TakeTask. Share happiness with your friends may bring you more rewards!

More

TakeTask wants you to take task happily and conveniently, so the application offers you all the optimized functions.

Map view of tasks list is provided and TakeTask will recommend tasks around you, let you to earn in a convenient way.

TakeTask also suggests the task completion/reporting time to let you have more control on taking the task.

TakeTask automatically obtains free WiFi information nearby. So take tasks freely and cost nothing!
